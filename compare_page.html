Description

This is the 258-litre upright freezer from Haier, it has a solid white finish and a capacity best suited to a medium to large family. It has a stylish black LED control panel on the exterior where you can easily monitor temperature and adjust settings on the fly. The assist handle provides quick and easy access into the interior so that you can grab your frozen items without hassle. There are six storage bins within the freezer, two of them are extra large and capable of storing your large and bulky items like frozen meat and ice cream. Inside the door is a handy pizza box compartment, where you can place your pizza for quick access without having to search throughout the other compartments.

This upright freezer has Super Freezer technology meaning it can quickly freeze items after a large grocery shop for better preservation. You won’t get lost inside this freezer with the handly LED lighting and you won’t have to worry about frost build up since it’s been designed frost free. This upright freezer is backed with a 2-year parts and labour warranty from Haier for added peace of mind.
